# Signal Criteria And Rankings

## Purpose

Define what counts as an atlas entry and how entries are ranked.

## Ranking Categories

- `candidate mechanism trace`
- `supporting correspondence`
- `direct mechanism exposition`
- `tool-derived candidate signal`
- `tooling lens`
- `background or provenance`
- `excluded or too tenuous`

## Working Definitions

## Mechanism-First Measuring Stick

For this consolidation pass, atlas ranking should be judged against the following working mechanism:

1. a stuck point, blockage, contradiction, overload, or unresolved tension
2. vaulting, deferral, compression, routing-around, or latent storage
3. a later pivot, return, changed angle, changed scale, or changed context
4. a catalyst or topology fit that makes the stuck material metabolizable
5. a rapid unlock, compounding integration, or eureka cascade
6. evidence that this pattern is structurally central in the source rather than decorative metaphor

Sources should not rank highly merely because they are:

- cross-domain
- analogy-shaped
- Symbound-adjacent
- thematically interesting

### `candidate mechanism trace`

Existing source material documents enough of the sequence that the mechanism can be reconstructed with uncertainty labels, even if the source does not state the final theory vocabulary directly.

### `supporting correspondence`

A source that maps onto some of the mechanism, but not enough to carry the argument.

### `direct mechanism exposition`

A source that explicitly explains part of the hypothesis machinery itself. This usually belongs mainly to repo one rather than as independent atlas evidence.

### `tool-derived candidate signal`

A candidate signal that comes from an existing tool output rather than from ordinary explanatory prose.

### `tooling lens`

A tool or method that may help surface mechanism traces or correspondences, but does not itself count as signal evidence.

### `background or provenance`

A source that helps explain origin, packaging, infrastructure, or method context but should not be treated as part of the atlas body.

### `excluded or too tenuous`

A source whose connection is too weak, too indirect, too ecosystem-wide, or too dependent on creative interpretation to include in the main body.

## Drafting Rule

When uncertain between two rankings, choose the weaker ranking.

## Provisional Rule

All current atlas cards should be treated as provisional until they have been checked directly against this mechanism-first measuring stick.
