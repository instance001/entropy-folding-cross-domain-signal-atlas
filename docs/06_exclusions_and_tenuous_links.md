# Exclusions And Tenuous Links

## Purpose

Record what was screened out of the main body and why.

## Material That Belongs Here

- too-weak correspondences
- ecosystem-adjacent repos with no direct textual bridge
- tooling that should remain tooling
- packaging or doctrine that should remain background

## Material That Must Not Go Here

- defensive argument for why excluded material secretly matters
- re-promotion of excluded sources into the atlas body

## Drafting Rule

Being excluded from the main body does not imply a source is valueless. It only means it does not meet the atlas threshold.
