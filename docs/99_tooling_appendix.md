# Tooling Appendix

## Purpose

Catalogue tools that may have generated or helped identify candidate correspondences.

These tools are not themselves proof of the hypothesis.

## Material That Belongs Here

- tool purpose
- what the tool was intended to surface
- what source material says about it
- whether existing outputs are present
- whether the tool remains only a proposed method

## Material That Must Not Go Here

- new discovery runs
- new theory generation
- promotion of methods into evidence

## Tooling Boundary Rule

The presence of a tool in this appendix means only that the source corpus contains a method, lens, or instrument that may surface candidate structure.

It does not mean:

- the tool validated the hypothesis
- the tool validated the atlas entries
- the tool's outputs carry stronger evidential weight than their source context warrants
